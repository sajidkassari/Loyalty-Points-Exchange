Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are loyalty points?

Loyalty points are rewards given to customers for their purchases, which can be redeemed for discounts or products.

How can I redeem my points?

You can redeem your points during the checkout process on our platform.

Who can use this system?

Both individual users and companies can log in to manage their accounts and points.
